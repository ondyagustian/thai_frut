ABOUT THAIFRUT STORE

Thai FRUT is the official company providing fresh sliced Fruits from Thailand , from Famous Green Thai Mango to the exotic Yellow watermelons. The Group has been working in this field since 2001 in UAE and serving people with high quality fruits carefully picked from our Farms to serve you with only the best.

We server more than 10 varities of fresh fruits through out the year, and always updated with seasonal fruits for the cravings. Our customized fruit bouquets are special for any occassion. Visit us and experience exotic thail flavour like never before.
